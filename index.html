What is Quick?

The youth of today are growing up in an environment where their parents are preoccupied in their fast-paced work and some children are unfortunate to look for this attention in negative ways. Some join gangs to get the attention they deserve, causing bad influences to ruin their lives.

"Quick" is a solution designed to keep young kids (ages 10-14) away from gang life by giving them a purpose and something to feel proud about. Children are motivated to do simple, yet appropriate tasks for some money and develop good work habits, as well as find meaning in their lives. We aim to give purpose and a easy job to kids who might otherwise turn to a life of crime.
